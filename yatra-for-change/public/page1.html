About

Join us at the our charitable trust in the serene environment of Palampur, Himachal Pradesh. We are a dedicated mental health and rehabilitation organization focused on providing care, psychological support, and community wellness. As a volunteer, you will assist our professional team with daily operations, community outreach, and digital or offline awareness campaigns. This is a unique opportunity to gain hands-on experience in the mental health sector while making a meaningful impact in a peaceful, supportive setting.
