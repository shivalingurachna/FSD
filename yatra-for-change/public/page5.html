About

I'm an entrepreneur running a small backpackers hostel in Kerala with my brother. We are starting a new property by the end of September and are looking for volunteers with love for art to help us bring life and color to our beach house through wall art and paintings. This is more than just a project — it’s an opportunity to leave your creative mark in a space where ocean and artistry meet.

✨ What we’re looking for:
Artists, painters, or hobbyists who enjoy expressing through wall art. A flair for playful, coastal-inspired themes (think seashells, sunsets, marine life, waves, tropical vibes). Passion and positivity — no formal experience required, just creativity and heart.
