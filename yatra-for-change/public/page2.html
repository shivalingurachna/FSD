About

Open for female volunteers ONLY. Volunteers are expected to follow our daily schedules and actively take part in our home's activities and routines. We value consistency, presence and a respectful approach to the structure and values we follow.
Given the sensitive backgrounds of the girls, it's important that volunteers engage with care and understanding.
Most importantly we need every volunteer to protect and prioritize the privacy of our girls here.

PLEASE NOTE:
- We follow a purely vegetarian diet and encourage you to do the same while you are here with us.
- Strictly prohibited from smoking/drinking or consumption of other drugs during your volunteering tenure in or outside the premises.
- We have 7 pet dogs who are rescues and have been an integral part of our lives. So make sure you are comfortable with them wandering around, (They are very friendly)
