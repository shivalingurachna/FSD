About

We are a social enterprise, working with women farmers and artisans of Uttarakhand Himalayas. By contributing your skills to enhance our brand presence on digital platform, you'll enjoy complimentary accommodation and meals (of course, delicious!), explore the picturesque hills of Uttarakhand, and engage in enriching interactions with your hosts. If you are confident with your skills hit that apply button and we'll have a complimentary kayak ride waiting for you at the Bhimtal lake 😉
